Here are the mediums I prefer to use when creating art:

  1. Oil Paint
    • I love using oil paint because of it's slow drying nature. It also blends well and makes it easier to paint realistic pictures.
    • A downside of using oil paint would also haveto be it's slow drying nature - it takes longer to paint between layers.
  2. Watercolor
    • I like using watercolor because it dries easily - paintings can be finished in the least amount of time.
    • I think a downside of using this medium would be that it allows for few mistakes + framing your work's a hassle.
