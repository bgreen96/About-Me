Patricia Cang, 25

Patricia Cang is a self-taught artist from Cagayan de Oro City. Her art focuses on human emotions, mental health, and the workings of the mind.

My paintings focus on human experiences and emotions. Nothing is constant in life and we’re never the same versions of ourselves. The complexities of the human mind have always intrigued me and through the years, I’ve found that it’s been the same thing that’s constantly been pushing me forward. As humans, we’re constantly evolving – our feelings change in a split second and our minds wander off more times than we’d like. There is no switch to make us feel happy or sad. Our thoughts cannot be controlled, regardless of how hard we may try to do so. At the end of the day, we’re always left with ourselves. We may not always like the version we’re at, but when all is silent, we are left with no one else.
